# Companion — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 13, 2026

Companion is a Chrome extension that helps you understand YouTube videos by
displaying personalized AI-generated explanations in a side panel.

## What we collect

We collect nothing. Companion has no servers and no backend. There is no
account system, no telemetry, and no analytics.

## What is stored locally on your device

The following are stored in your browser's local extension storage
(`chrome.storage.local`) on your device only:

- Your API keys for the AI provider(s) you have chosen to use
- Your preferences (profile, theme, output language, auto-close settings)
- Cached analyses of videos you have analyzed (transcripts and AI output)
- Terms you have marked as "already know" or "more like this"

None of this leaves your device except as described below.

## What is sent to your chosen AI provider

When you click "Analyze this video" or while live rolling analysis is active,
Companion sends the following directly from your browser to the AI provider
you have configured:

- The transcript of the active YouTube video
- Your profile description and language preference
- Your API key (used to authenticate the request)

The provider's response (cards, summaries, chat replies) is returned directly
to your browser and displayed in the side panel.

Your data, once sent to your chosen provider, is governed by that provider's
privacy policy:

- Anthropic: <https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy>
- OpenAI: <https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy>
- Google (Gemini): <https://policies.google.com/privacy>
- DeepSeek: <https://deepseek.com/privacy>

We have no visibility into or control over what those providers do with the
transcript. Companion does not log, store, or transmit any of this data to
us — because there is no "us." Everything is between your browser and the
provider you chose.

## Permissions

Companion requests the following Chrome permissions:

- **storage** — to save preferences and the analysis cache
- **sidePanel** — to display the side panel UI
- **activeTab, scripting, tabs** — to read the transcript and seek the player
- **host access to youtube.com** — to read the transcript and control the player
- **host access to the four AI provider domains** — to send your transcript to
  the provider you selected

## Limited Use compliance

The use of information received by Companion adheres to the Chrome Web Store
User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

- Data is used only to provide Companion's single purpose (analyzing YouTube
  videos at the user's request)
- Data is not sold or transferred to third parties for unrelated purposes
- Data is not used to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes
- Data is not used for personalized advertising

## Children

Companion is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
data from anyone, including children.
